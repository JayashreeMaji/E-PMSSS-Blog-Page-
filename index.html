Application Timings

This scholarship is provided to the applicants every year. The time of registration starts from September for 5 consecutive days.

  • 05/09 - 10/09

    Scholarship Money

    A lumpsum amount of Rs 30,000 is provided to the applicants per semester to help them with their studies and throughout the period of graduation a amount of Rs 1 Lakh is provided per year till their graduation gets completed.
